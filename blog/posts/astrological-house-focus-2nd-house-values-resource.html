Feeling a little… squeezed? Like your resources are stretched thinner than ever before? August 2025 is bringing a potent spotlight to our 2nd Houses, the astrological domain of values, finances, possessions, and self-worth. This isn't just about money, though that's certainly part of it. Your 2nd House represents your relationship with your resources – both tangible and intangible. This month, the challenge lies in aligning our Leo-fueled ambitions with the cautious, reflective energy of Mercury retrograde and the waning moon's call to release what no longer serves us.
