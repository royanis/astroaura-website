Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about AstroAura's cosmic features and functionality.

How accurate is the AI cosmic chat compared to human astrologers?

Our AI is trained on extensive astrological knowledge and your personal birth chart data. While it provides highly personalized and accurate insights, it complements rather than replaces human intuition. The AI offers consistent, accessible guidance based on traditional astrological principles combined with your unique cosmic blueprint.

What birth information do I need for accurate readings?

For the most accurate natal chart and personalized readings, you'll need your exact birth date, time (as precise as possible), and location. If you don't know your exact birth time, AstroAura can still provide valuable insights using your date and location, though some features may be less precise.

How does the tarot integration work with astrology?

AstroAura's tarot readings are enhanced by your astrological profile. The AI considers both traditional card meanings and your unique cosmic influences to provide deeper, more relevant interpretations. This integration offers a more comprehensive spiritual guidance experience.

Can I use AstroAura for relationship compatibility?

Yes! AstroAura offers comprehensive compatibility analysis by comparing natal charts (synastry) and creating composite charts. You can explore romantic relationships, friendships, or business partnerships to understand your cosmic connections and potential challenges.

How often are the transit predictions updated?

Transit data is updated in real-time based on current planetary positions. You'll receive notifications about significant transits affecting your chart, and can view both current influences and upcoming cosmic events for planning purposes.
